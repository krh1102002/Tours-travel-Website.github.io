Ajanta and Ellora Caves

₹  10,000

Description

Location: Northern Maharashtra, around 400 kilometers (250 miles) from Mumbai.
when to visit: The best time to visit the caves is from November to March, when it's cooler and dry.
More About Place: You can get an excellent view and perspective of inside the Kailasa Temple at Ellora by climbing up the hill around it. When visiting Ajanta, ask your driver to drop you at the viewpoint and meet you at the car park. Walk downhill from the viewpoint and enter at cave eight. From there, walk right to the end and start at cave 28. This will enable you to see nearly half the caves without the crowds. Bring a torch with you to the caves as many of them are quite dark and lighting is poor. Try to reach the caves before 10 a.m. to beat the crowds and tour buses.
